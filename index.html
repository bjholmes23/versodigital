WHAT DO WE DO?

Our Services

We have a few tricks up our sleeves. It all depends on what you need to get your product up and running

Tech Leadership & Consulting

Hiring senior tech talent is a serious investment for an early-stage startup. We provide guidance, strategic planning and code reviews on a flexible part-time basis.

Development & Production

We specialise in app and web development, responsive mobile solutions as well as demanding backend builds. Node.js-based APIs and complex Docker deployments are our forte.

Interface Design

Great interfaces are not just about pretty pixels. We create interfaces that are delightful as well as intuitive for your users.

User Experience

We build products on a solid foundation of user research and testing, solving design challenges dynamically towards a bespoke experience for your users.

Our work
